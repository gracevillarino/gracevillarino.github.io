Since WordPress is open source, there are hundreds of resources available for training. From WordPress videos and podcasts to blogs and books, you can easily teach yourself WordPress if desired. Unfortunately not everyone has time to comb through website after website looking for help. For these situations, one-on-one WordPress training is great option that delivers quick and sustainable results.

We’ll even walk you through your WordPress admin panel and help you configure WordPress or add plugins during our training session.

  • Installing or Upgrading WordPress
  • Optimizing, Repairing, and Backing Up the Database
  • Standard Configuring Options and Settings
  • Locating a WordPress Theme

  • Creating Pages and Posts
